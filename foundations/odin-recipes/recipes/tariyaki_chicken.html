tariyaki chicken

Ingredients: Chicken thighs Soy sauce Mirin Sake Sugar Grated ginger and garlic Instructions: In a bowl, combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and grated ginger and garlic to create the teriyaki sauce. Marinate chicken thighs in the teriyaki sauce for at least 30 minutes. Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken thighs on each side until fully cooked, basting with additional teriyaki sauce. Once cooked, slice the chicken and serve with steamed rice and vegetables.
