ramen

Ingredients: Ramen noodles Broth (chicken, pork, or vegetable) Sliced pork belly or chicken Ramen eggs (soft-boiled marinated eggs) Green onions Nori strips Instructions: Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a separate pot, heat the chosen broth. Add thinly sliced pork belly or chicken and simmer until cooked through. In serving bowls, assemble the ramen by placing the cooked noodles and ladling the hot broth with meat over them. Top with ramen eggs cut in half, chopped green onions, and nori strips. Serve immediately, and optionally, provide soy sauce and chili oil on the side for extra flavor.
