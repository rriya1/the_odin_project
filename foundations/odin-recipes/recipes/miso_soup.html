miso soup

Ingredients: Miso paste Dashi (fish or seaweed stock) Tofu, cubed Wakame seaweed Green onions, chopped Instructions: In a pot, heat the dashi stock until hot but not boiling. Add cubed tofu and wakame seaweed. Take a small amount of miso paste and dissolve it in a ladle with some of the hot stock. Add it back to the pot and stir well. Simmer the soup for a few minutes, being careful not to boil it as high heat can destroy the miso's flavor. Garnish with chopped green onions just before serving.
